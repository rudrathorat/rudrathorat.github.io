WHO ARE WE

We are a organisation who believe in firmly adapting to today's lifestyle changes and helping people to deal with various stages. From unempoyment due to COVID 19 pandemic or education to the student and providing healthcare to the people. We at Urban Hope are the set of people committed to work extensively.

Our efforts

CATERING UNEMPLOYMENT Many companies or employees will be able to go back to their work after this Unlock is properly completed. Now, these companies have been reliant on the government protection scheme, but for how long, at some point subsidies wither out, then the companies have to lay off their employees in one way or another. This will actually accelerate the unemployment rate at different levels. Our website Urban Hope provides people suffering from this pandemic job opportunities and are trying to teach people with various techniques and methods that are useful in this type of INDIAN market . With thorough research team we try to find the best out of the people wo apply with us and try to find out the strengths and weaknesses of different people. PROVIDING HEALTHCARE IN COVID 19 The diversity of India is reflected in the Covid journey of states, which are at different levels of infection, response and recovery. Ten states currently account for 90% of India’s infections. Behind the scenes, however, India’s federal structure is at play with centralized policy decision and action guiding decentralized and contextualized response in the states. EDUCATION FOR EVERYONE Education is the most important thing in a students career and after the COVID-19 virus the education system has been affected . But people are trying to find its alternatives by going online and looking for courses that are important for their careers . Our website Urban hope provides information information about the various courses that are beneficial and are important to learn in this present market and we show the students where to access these courses for free
